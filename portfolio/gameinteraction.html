Shapes

November 9, 2024

A game called "Shapes" tells a story of a circle. The main shape is the circle, which is the only circle to exist, surrounded by other shapes like squares and triangles. Its goal is to make it through to the end. There are obstacles it has to avoid throughout the levels of different shapes. Being the only circle, it wants to determine why it differs from the other shapes.
