Jellyfish on land or UFO?

Jellyfish are interesting creatures yet simple, with no brain, heart, or bone. They are also immortal and can cheat death by regenerating their organs. Maybe they are aliens? The ocean is a strange place with many creatures to explore. I chose bright colors for my piece since most jellyfish come in many colors and different shapes and patterns.
