Assignment #2

September 27, 2024

For this assignment I wanted to mainly focus on sounds and the emotion it is creating. I wanted to have a calm feeling in the beginning then created an incline of dramatic leading back to a calm feeling in the ending. Some parts of the sounds I purposely wanted to have faded to create anticipation For the visuals I wanted to have a lot of nature and sceneries. In the beginning I wanted to have the sense of the birds flying and then showing flowers, giving the idea that the birds are looking at the ground. Same thing with the ending except I added an additional clip of the tiger walking and the background changes season as time goes by. For the dramatic scene I wanted to add a clip of a tiger staring to give it an ominous feeling like there being a watch. I also intentionally added gaps between clips the same thing with the sound to create a more dramatic feeling.
