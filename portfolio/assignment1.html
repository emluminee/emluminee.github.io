Assignment #1

This video intentionally have no aduio

September 6, 2024

In my video I want to show a crossover between a game and real life. There is a loading screen that opens in the introduction showing an E for everyone that gives a feeling of nostalgia. I wanted to create a fast paced video showing that it was an advertisement but also showing that it was a slice of life. In the video there is a mouse and a cat that both want to make a cake and there are fireworks to celebrate them baking a cake. The texts that happen between help guide the story on what the mouse and cat are doing. There is also a black and white clip with the mouse and cat having flashbacks of what happened before or what could happen next. The ending of the video shows the advertisement for the game and a short clip after resembling the slice of life.
