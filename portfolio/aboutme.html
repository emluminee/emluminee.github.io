Meet Emily

Emily is a transfer junior at San Jose State University (SJSU) and is an alumnus of Southwestern College. Her major is studio arts but wants to pursue graphic design and illustrations.

Emily enjoys other art media, including photography, filming, drawing, and many more. She also enjoys trying new things. Some of Emily's favorite things to do besides art are going to the beach, listening to music, and dancing.
