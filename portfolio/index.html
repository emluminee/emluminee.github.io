About Me

Meet Emily

Emily Chaing is an aspiring artist who started with only a pencil and paper at a young age. She was fascinated about art at a young age witch curious her to wanting to learn more about it. In the beginning of her art journey she was mostly self taught and learn about tradional art through her creative passion such as sculpting, painting, and her favorite drawing. She never knew the many styles and forms of art until she stumbled upon animations on youtube and watching Disney. Now with the creative skills she haved learn throughout her journey and still continue to do so, her interpretation of art is to create an combination of digital media and traditional arts in creating something meaningful that represents and shows an impact art can create in society and nature.
