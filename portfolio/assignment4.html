October 24, 2024

For this assignment we got to create our own video from scratch with our own recording and own audio. For my video I chose to do my topic on time. I wanted to use time to show the moment of a day going by that can lead to itself reappearing over and over again in a loop. I used my camera to record my clips and audio. Some of the main clips I have recorded are clocks, plants movements, and walking up the stairs. In the editing of the video I used some of the same clips throughout the video. I add some of the same clips like the clock in the beginning, I add a reverse showing time going backwards as well as the th stairs I also add a reverse. Then towards the end the same clock and stairs I did not add a reverse leading back to the beginning.
